Asher's Chocolate Cake

Chocolate Cake with Buttermilk Icing

What You'll Need

  • 1 cup of butter
  • 2 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 2 egss
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup of cocoa
  • 2 1/2 cup of flour
  • 1/2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp of baking soda
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 2 cup of buttermilk
  • (make from powdered buttermilk)

Steps to Make

  1. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy
  2. Add eggs and vanilla
  3. Blend well
  4. Combine cocoa, dry buttermilk, flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt
  5. Add to cream mixture
  6. Bake: preheat to 350 degrees

About the Recipe

This chocolate cake with buttermilk icing brings something to the table for everyone. It has a rich, chocolatey flavor that is great for chocolate lovers - balanced by a light buttermilk icing. Its a moist, spongy cake that is hard to put down! When I was a kid, my grandmother used to make this cake every year for my birthday, and its still my favorite cake to this day. I have fond memories of making it with her at her house around the holidays. I knew it as "grandma's cake", but everyone else in my family knew it as "Asher's favorite cake." Hopefully you can share in this wonderful recipe with your loved ones too. :)
